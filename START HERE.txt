INSURANCE CRM — how to run it (no terminal needed)
====================================================

EVERYDAY USE
------------
1. Double-click  "Start CRM"  (or the "Insurance CRM" icon on your Desktop).
2. A black window opens and your browser opens the app at http://localhost:3000.
3. KEEP THE BLACK WINDOW OPEN while you use the app.
4. When you're done for the day, close the black window to shut it down.

(If you double-click it again while it's already running, it just re-opens
 the app in your browser — it won't start a second copy.)


ONE-TIME: PUT AN ICON ON YOUR DESKTOP
-------------------------------------
Double-click  "Create Desktop Shortcut"  once. After that you can launch the
app from the "Insurance CRM" icon on your Desktop.

(Optional) To make it start by itself every time you turn on the PC:
  - Press Windows key + R, type   shell:startup   and press Enter.
  - Copy the "Insurance CRM" Desktop icon into the folder that opens.


AFTER THE APP HAS BEEN UPDATED
------------------------------
If your developer changes the app, double-click  "Update CRM"  once,
then start it as usual.


WANT TO UNDERSTAND THE WHOLE APP?
---------------------------------
Open the illustrated guide:  docs\GUIDE.html
(double-click it — it opens in your browser with screenshots of every screen,
 the everyday flows, and exactly how/where your data is stored.)


GOOD TO KNOW
------------
- Everything stays on THIS computer. Your client data never leaves the PC.
- The AI features and WhatsApp use your own keys/number that are already set up.
- If anything looks wrong, close the black window and double-click "Start CRM"
  again. If it still fails, send a photo of the black window to your developer.
